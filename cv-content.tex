\newcommand{\userbio}{%
  PhD researcher combining information theory and nonparametric statistics with attention-based architectures. 
  Introduced Nonparametric Variational Information Bottleneck (NVIB), a novel approach that models Transformer 
  embeddings as nonparametric mixture distributions for better interpretability, regularisation, and generative modelling. 
  Incoming postdoctoral researcher at the University of Oxford with the Torr Vision Group, focusing on AI for legal modelling.
